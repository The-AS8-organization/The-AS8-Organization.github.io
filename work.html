The AS8 Organization

Learn

Our curriculum lives in the cloud, continuously updating itself from domain experts around the world. We designed a framework on top of the curriculum specifically designed to get you hired! We asked hundreds of top tech companies what specific skills they look for in candidates including skills for the modern workplace like critical thinking, problem-solving, collaboration, and creativity.

Share

We built an exclusive community of like-minded people to help you learn and grow. These connected geeks share resources, experiences, and perspectives through blogs, tutorials, etc. They're there to help you when you're stuck or feeling low. A community is about people having close relationships with each other & we encourage our members to do so.

Jam

The modern workplace doesn't just require domain expertise but also the ability to work in teams to achieve shared goals. To improve the necessary soft-skills of the workplace we encourage community members to conduct brainstorming sessions, talks, discussions workshops in various cities with unlimited coffee, food, and music.

Create

Learning is most effective when you're able to apply it on projects. We inspire to build/innovate new products which can enhance or improve the quality of life in exponential ways. You'll collaborate with other students on a group project and build a production-ready app with a team of students from other disciplines.

Work

Now that you're confident with yourself, it's time to show the world what you know and can do. We connect with top recruiters around the world for oppotunites in hiring our community members. We take a small cut from your income to keep us motivated and keep innovating in your learning experience in the community.

The AS8 Organization

Who we are?

We're a team of lifelong learners, an enthusiast community to support individuals through the lifelong learning revolution.

In other words, "The AS8 organization" is a community for Bangladeshi developers to collaborate on projects, connect with each-others, and grow together. There are tons of resources on the internet, but most of the typical Bangladeshi students find it difficult to start their programming journey. We are here to help you out by providing necessary resources, mentorship, and organizing code jams, hackathons, and different events throughout the year. Our mission is to inspire a lifelong love of learning in innovative ways and to create a learning experience that is fun, fruitful, and rewarding.
